        Description

        Fresh


        Fresh is the debut of our new product line, Custom Themes. We're using a quality > quantity approach, with a goal of giving icons familiar yet fresh designs. Each Fresh icon is handcrafted to have a consistent playful feel, with creative designs and detail.


        Features


        • Premium, handcrafted icons

        • Playful, creative wallpapers

        • Weekly updates with new icons

        • Full dynamic calendar support (day and date)


        Updates and Requests


        We're committed to adding new icons on a weekly basis. Those icons will consist of popular Android apps and games, as well as misc. icons that can be used for unthemed icons.

        Fresh Icons can take a significant amount of time to design, so we're only focusing on adding in popular app and game icons. For that reason, icon requests are not available at this time.


        Supported Launchers


        • Nova, Apex, Action, Smart, Aviate, Trebuchet, Next, Atom, KK, Solo, Lucid, Inspire, Nine, ADW, Holo, GO, TSF, Themer

        • Unicon, XGELS, CM Theme Engine
